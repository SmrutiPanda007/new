How grpTalk works

Can I receive a call from grpTalk if I don’t have my cellular data turned on or an active internet connection?

3G or internet is not required to receive a call. However cellular data (3G or faster) or internet is required to schedule a call and to control the call using the app.

Do my friends and family need grpTalk app to receive my grpTalk calls?

App is not required to receive a call from grpTalk. However, to host your own grpTalk you need to download the app.

Can I add a contact to grpTalk which is not in my phone contact book?

Yes, you can add a number to the live call using a “dial pad”. Once your call is live, open the grpTalk app, select the live group call (manage), click on “Add member” icon and choose “dial pad”. Type in number and press “call”, it will be added to the live call.

What should I do if I missed a grpTalk call?

You can always join a grpTalk by calling that number you got a missed call from as long as the talk is ongoing.

Why is there no PIN to join a grpTalk?

You can join a grpTalk only if you are invited. Therefore no PIN is necessary.

Does my network charge me for receiving a call from grpTalk?

Since incoming calls are free, you will not be charged.

Will other members be able to participate in the grpTalk without the host joining?

Yes, other members can start the grpTalk without the host.

If my call is disconnected due to some reason, while my grpTalk is in progress, how can I connect to my live grpTalk?

To reconnect you can always call the grpTalk number you received a call from.

Can I use my account on multiple phones? Will I lose my grpTalk history?

No, you can only use it on one phone at a time. If you activate your account on another phone your account will be deactivated on the old phone and grpTalk history will be transferred to the new phone.

What if some of my contacts are on DND, will grpTalk still be able to call them?

If some of the members in your group are on DND, you (host) will be prompted to send them an SMS from your mobile asking them to opt-in. Only those that opt-in will receive a call from grpTalk at scheduled time.

Can I call other countries using grpTalk?

Currently you can only call members within the country. International calling is coming soon.

Features

How many people can I talk to using grpTalk?

By default you can talk to maximum of 20 members at a time. If you would like to increase lines please contact us at hello@grptalk.com.

How do I selectively mute participants on the call?

Yes, using the mute button against every member’s name in the live grpTalk panel. You can also mute all members at once using the ‘mute all’ button above the host name.

Can I add or remove members as a host, while grpTalk is in progress?

Yes, a host can add or remove members while grpTalk is in progress.

How do I record my grpTalk? Can I download the recordings?

Yes, you can turn on or off grpTalk recording whenever you want. You can download individual recordings in that grpTalk’s history tab. By default this feature will be disabled for your account, if you would like to subscribe, please contact us at hello@grptalk.com.

Where can I see detailed reports of my past grpTalks?

You can view them in the ‘History’ tab of a particular grpTalk. Access details of members that participated, recordings, call charges and more.

Can I schedule a call for next month?

Yes, using the schedule feature.

I scheduled a call for tomorrow and instead started the call today. Does it affect my scheduled call?

No. Your scheduled grpTalk for tomorrow will happen as planned unless you cancel it.

grpTalk Web Access

What is grpTalk web?

It is a web based application and simply an extension of your phone, which gives you access to your existing grpTalk account. All your data including your mobile contacts and groups are fully synced between your phone and your computer. Any action you take on the phone will apply to grpTalk on your computer and vice versa.

How do I use grpTalk Web?

GrpTalk Web is not another grpTalk account. When you use grpTalk on your computer and your phone, you are simply accessing the same account on these two devices.

Minimum requirements to enjoy GrpTalk Web
• You need to have an active GrpTalk app on your phone.
• You need to have a stable internet connection on both your phone and your computer.

To get started with grpTalk Web
1. Visit web.grptalk.com on your computer.
2. Open grpTalk on your phone. In the groups home screen > Menu > grpTalk Web.
3. Scan the QR Code on your computer screen from your phone.

From your phone, navigate to grpTalk Web to view your logged in computers or to logout from an active grpTalk Web session.

How do I log out of an active grpTalk session on my computer from my phone?

You can log out of grpTalk on your computer even if you are not physically near your computer.

To do so:
Open grpTalk on your mobile phone > > Open Menu > grpTalk Web > > Log out from this computer.
Once you have tapped on Log out from this computer, any previously established logins will no longer be valid. You will have to scan the QR code the next time you open grpTalk on your computer.

Call Charges & Payment

Why am I being charged for grpTalk?

grpTalk calls members on their mobile phones. Therefore regular carrier charges along with a minimum service cost will apply.

If I host a grpTalk with 4 members for 10 minutes, how many minutes will I be charged?

4 members x 10 m = 40 m
1 host x 10 m = 10 m
Total 50 minutes (provided everyone is called by grpTalk at the same time)

How do I make a payment? How can I recharge my account?

You can recharge your account through in-app purchases. Open MENU >> Recharge, it will guide you through.

How do I check my balance?

You can see your account balance in the Account Tab in Main Menu.

Advanced Settings

What is “Dial In Only”?

By enabling this feature all dial out (grpTalk server calling all the members) will be stopped, and at the time of the conference all members need to dial in/call to the grpTalk number to join the conference. No PIN is required to join the conference as all the members will be joined to the conference automatically once they call the conference number.

What is “Allow Non-Members”?

You can give access to Non-Members (who were not added to the conference group) to join the conference by dialing to the grpTalk number and entering the PIN. PIN will be included in the “conference alert SMS”-sent to you when you schedule a conference and in “conference reminder SMS” -sent to you 30 minutes before the conference scheduled time.

Can I select “Dial In Only” and “Allow Non-Members” features together?

Yes you can! When you do so -

  • All the members (who were added to the conference group) can join the conference by dialing to the grpTalk number without any PIN.
  • All the Non-Members (who were not added to the conference group) can join the conference by dialing to the grpTalk number and entering provided PIN for authentication.

Why is a PIN required for a Non-Member to join the conference? Can’t he/she join the conference without a PIN?

No, he/she can’t! We use PIN to verify the Non-Member’s authentication to join the conference, and we also use it to recognize the conference they need to join..

What is “Mute Dial”?

When you select this feature, all the participants (Dial In & Dial out) will be connected to the conference in MUTE except the “Host”; and whenever needed, the host can UNMUTE the whole conference or a specific participant. This feature is helpful for the conferences with more than 10 participants. You can select this feature along with any advanced settings.

What is “Open line before 30 minutes”?

You can use this feature when you schedule a conference. The conference lines will be opened 30 minutes before the conference scheduled time. If you want a private chat with any selected members, you can ask them to dial in to the conference before it actually starts.
